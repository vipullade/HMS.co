Our Services

✻

Harmon M. Shragge & Company specializes in the investment and management of commercial and industrial properties for private clients. We focus on light industrial, retail and office investments with values in the $3,000,000 to $15,000,000 range (above the "mom and pops" but below the pension funds and REIT's). We currently own and/or manage properties ranging in size from 1,200 to 120,424 square feet throughout Northern California with a total market value of approximately $80 million.

Located in downtown San Francisco, our hands-on approach coupled with our extensive contacts in cities throughout Northern California enables us to effectively handle all aspects of asset and property management in the cities in which our properties are located: San Jose, Foster City, San Luis Obispo, San Francisco, Rio Vista, and Pleasanton.

About Us

∎

Harmon M. Shragge & Company was established in 1975 by Harmon M. Shragge Sr. (1924-2002), and has since been owned and managed by Harmon M. Shragge Jr. In the late 1970's and early 1980's, the firm's primary focus was on the sales, purchases and leasing of existing buildings, as well as build-to-suits of Goodyear Tires and Safeway stores. In the late 1990's Harmon M. Shragge & Company added to its scope of services seismic retrofitting and the redevelopment of depreciated properties. In 2002, Harmon Shragge Jr. incorporated the company.
